Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Besties, Inc. ("Besties," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to the safety and protection of all users, particularly minors. This page outlines our standards and practices for preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on the Besties™ platform.

1. Platform Age Restriction

Besties™ is designed exclusively for college students aged 18 and older. Our Terms of Service explicitly require that all users be at least 18 years of age to create an account or use the platform. Users who are found to be under 18 are in violation of our Terms and will have their accounts immediately terminated.

Additionally, all users must verify their identity using a valid .edu email address before accessing the platform. This university email verification requirement serves as an additional safeguard to prevent minors from accessing the app.

2. Zero Tolerance Policy

Besties maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any form of child exploitation. Any content or behavior that sexualizes, exploits, endangers, or harms minors is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate action, including but not limited to:

  • Immediate removal of the offending content
  • Permanent suspension of the offending account
  • Reporting to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) via the CyberTipline
  • Reporting to relevant law enforcement authorities

3. Reporting Mechanisms

Besties provides multiple ways for users to report child safety concerns:

  • In-App Reporting: Users can report any profile, post, or message by tapping the report button within the app. Reports related to child safety are prioritized for immediate review.
  • Email: Users can report concerns directly to our safety team at support@allmybesties.com.

4. Content Moderation

We employ a combination of proactive and reactive measures to detect and prevent CSAE content on our platform:

  • Automated content moderation systems to detect and flag prohibited content
  • User-generated reports reviewed by our team
  • Regular audits of platform content and user activity
  • Prompt removal of any content that violates our policies

5. Cooperation with Authorities

Besties complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations. We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and report any identified CSAM or child exploitation to the appropriate regional and national authorities, including but not limited to:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI)
  • Local and state law enforcement agencies as applicable

6. Data Preservation

When we identify potential CSAE content or receive a report related to child safety, we preserve all relevant data and evidence in accordance with legal requirements to support any investigation by law enforcement authorities.

7. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding our child safety practices, or to report a child safety issue, please contact us:

Besties, Inc.
Email: support@allmybesties.com

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update these child safety standards from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
