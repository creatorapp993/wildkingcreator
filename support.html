Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use Besties?

Besties is for college students. You must have a valid .edu email address to create an account.

How do I verify my account?

When you sign up with your .edu email, we'll send you a verification link. Click the link to verify your email and complete your account setup.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please email support@allmybesties.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

How do I report someone?

To report inappropriate behavior, harassment, or content that violates our community guidelines, email support@allmybesties.com with details about the issue. Include screenshots if possible.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings and within the Besties app settings. On iOS, go to Settings → Besties → Notifications. On Android, go to Settings → Apps → Besties → Notifications.

Can I change my email address?

Your email address is tied to your student identity and cannot be changed. If you need to use a different .edu email, you'll need to create a new account.

Community Guidelines

Besties is a community built on respect and genuine connection. We expect all users to:

  • Be respectful and kind to other users
  • Use your real identity and authentic photos
  • Not harass, bully, or intimidate others
  • Not post inappropriate, offensive, or illegal content
  • Not spam or misuse the platform
  • Report violations to help keep the community safe

Violations of these guidelines may result in account suspension or termination.
